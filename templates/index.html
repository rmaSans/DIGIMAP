What is this?

Add color to your grayscale image! The Colorful Image Colorizer (CIC) is a collection of 2 AI models that colorize grayscale images. Created by Zhang, R., et al.(2016 & 2017) Try it out!

Instructions

Look for the upload button and select your image. This ONLY works for JPG/JPEG images.

Wait for the Colorful Image Colorizer to do its job.

Download and enjoy your two colorized and upscaled images.
