How Colorful Image Colorizer Works

Colorful Image Colorization is an algorithm that takes in black & white photos and attacks the problem of attempting to add plausible color schemes to it. What makes it unique is that previous implementations have either relied on a significant amount of user interaction, or have resulted in desaturated colorizations. This algorithm however, proposes a fully automated approach to providing photographs a set of vibrant, yet realistic colorizations by using a feed-forward pass in a CNN whilst training the model with over a million existing images in order to be able choose what sets of colors would match well with specific images.


Through this web application, you are provided a more accessible way of adding color to your photo within just a few clicks. What happens behind the scenes however, is a bigger story. First the algorithm takes in the grayscale image you just uploaded, and then analyzes the objects in it. Based on the images it used in training, it then paints possible colors to the different areas of the image it thinks is the most appropriate. It then puts the colors on top of the image that you have provided, producing a new image just for you.
